About

ARKnowledgeForest is an Augmented Reality Application. Compared with traditional applications, we use a more interesting, vivid way to display knowledge and make learning more funny. Using Augmented Reality(AR), we visualize the Knowledge Forest with 3D form.

No matter which course or which book, what you need to do is open the APP, scanning any page of the book, then you can see all the key words are framed. Touching the key word you are interested in or want to learn, ARKnowledgeForest wiil automatically build the ARTree of the key word. And then, touch the little ball(knowledge fragments set), you can learn any knowledge fragment you want.

Having fun with learning！
